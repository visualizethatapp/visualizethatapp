Phepo Farmers
Phepo Farmers is an agritech app that connects backyard or small-scale farmers with customers. The plaform's goal is to help small-scale farmers make money from their produce and to assist potential customers in finding home grown agricultural products.
Challenge
Three Main Challenges
  • Households in rural areas grow seasonal vegetables for nutrition and food security. Oversupply of vegetables is shared among the community, missing the economic opportunity.
  • Small-scale food vendors spend an hour or two each day traveling to purchase the vegetables required for their businesses.
  • Private car taxis fail to reach daily income targets due to competition with 15-seater taxis.
Personas
Solution
Create a platform that connects backyard farmers with potential customers and facilitates delivery.
